# Rating Guide

The rating system lets BookVerse traders build a public reputation through honest feedback after each completed trade. This guide explains how ratings work, when and how to submit one, and what makes a fair and useful rating.

## Why Ratings Matter

BookVerse is a peer-to-peer trading platform — you are exchanging books with strangers. Ratings are the primary way the community establishes trust:

- **For receivers of proposals**: a trader's star average and rating count let you assess whether someone has a reliable history before accepting a trade.
- **For browsers**: a trader's profile shows their average rating, helping you decide whether to propose a trade.
- **For the community**: consistent, honest ratings deter poor behaviour and reward traders who communicate well and represent their books accurately.

A platform with accurate ratings works better for everyone. Inflated ratings hide problem traders; missing ratings leave the next person with no information.

## How the Rating System Works

### One Rating Per Trade, Per Party

Each completed trade unlocks one rating opportunity for each party. The proposer rates the receiver, and the receiver rates the proposer — two independent ratings, one per direction.

You cannot rate yourself. The system automatically assigns your rating to the other party in the trade.

### Ratings Are Permanent

Once submitted, a rating cannot be edited or deleted. Take time to write a fair and accurate assessment before submitting.

### Average Is Recalculated Live

Every time a new rating is submitted for a user, their average star rating and total rating count are recalculated across all their completed trades and updated on their profile immediately.

```
Profile: Jordan
★★★★☆  4.2 average · 15 ratings
```

## When You Can Rate

Rating becomes available after **both** of the following conditions are met:

1. The trade status is **completed** — either party has clicked **Mark as Complete** on the trade.
2. You have not already submitted a rating for this trade.

If the trade is still **proposed** or **accepted**, the rating form is not available. Complete the physical book exchange and mark the trade complete first.

## How to Submit a Rating

**Step 1** — Go to **My Trades** and open a completed trade.

```
┌──────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│  Trade with Jordan             ● completed   │
│                                              │
│  The Hobbit  ◀────────────────▶  Dune        │
│                                              │
│  [ Rate Jordan ]                             │
└──────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

**Step 2** — Click **Rate [trader name]**. The rating form opens.

```
┌──────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│  Rate Your Trading Partner                   │
│  Jordan                                      │
├──────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│  Stars                                       │
│                                              │
│  ☆ ☆ ☆ ☆ ☆                                  │
│  1   2   3   4   5                           │
│                                              │
│  Comment  (required for 3 stars or fewer)    │
│  ┌────────────────────────────────────────┐  │
│  │                                        │  │
│  └────────────────────────────────────────┘  │
│  Max 1000 characters                         │
│                                              │
│  [ Submit Rating ]                           │
└──────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

**Step 3** — Click a star (1–5) to set your rating.

**Step 4** — Write a comment. A comment is **required** if you select 3 stars or fewer — you must explain the issue. For 4–5 stars a comment is optional but encouraged.

**Step 5** — Click **Submit Rating**. The rating is saved and the trader's profile average updates immediately.

## Rating Scale

| Stars | Meaning | Typical experience |
|-------|---------|-------------------|
| ★★★★★ 5 | Excellent | Fast response, book exactly as described, smooth handoff, would trade again |
| ★★★★☆ 4 | Good | Minor issue (slight delay, small discrepancy) but overall positive |
| ★★★☆☆ 3 | Average | Noticeable problems — slow communication or condition slightly below listing |
| ★★☆☆☆ 2 | Poor | Significant issues — book condition substantially worse than described or very slow response |
| ★☆☆☆☆ 1 | Very poor | Serious problem — book did not arrive, fundamentally misrepresented, or no communication |

> **Comment required**: Ratings of 3 stars or below must include a comment. This ensures low ratings are substantiated and genuinely useful to the next trader.

## Writing a Comment

Comments are optional for 4–5 star ratings and required for 1–3 star ratings. Maximum 1000 characters.

### What to Include

A useful comment covers:

- **Communication** — Did the trader respond promptly? Were messages clear and friendly?
- **Book condition** — Did it match the listed condition (Like New / Good / Fair / Poor)?
- **Handoff** — Was the exchange arranged and completed smoothly?
- **Overall impression** — Would you trade with this person again?

### Examples of Good Comments

**5 stars:**
```
Fast to respond, book was in exactly the condition described — genuinely
Like New. Met on time at the agreed location. Would definitely trade again.
```

**4 stars:**
```
Took a day or two to reply but was friendly once we connected. Book was
Good condition as listed, though the spine had a bit more wear than the
photos showed. Smooth meetup overall.
```

**3 stars (comment required):**
```
Communication was slow — took four days to reply after acceptance. Book
arrived in Fair condition despite being listed as Good (noticeable
highlighting inside). Trade completed but I'd check carefully before
accepting from this trader again.
```

**2 stars (comment required):**
```
Book arrived with a cracked spine and torn pages, listed as Like New.
Contacted the trader and they did not respond. Would not trade again.
```

**1 star (comment required):**
```
Accepted the trade and then stopped responding entirely. Book was never
exchanged. Had to leave the trade in accepted status indefinitely.
Do not recommend.
```

### What to Avoid

- **Emotional or personal language** — Focus on the trading experience, not personal opinions.
- **Vague criticism without specifics** — "Bad trader" is not helpful. Describe what actually happened.
- **Retaliation** — Do not leave a low rating because the other party left you a low rating.
- **Abusive content** — Threats or harassment violate the Terms of Service.

## Rating Guidelines

### Rate Every Completed Trade

Even a simple 5-star rating with no comment is valuable. A trader with 30 ratings signals reliability far more than one with 0 ratings, regardless of how polished their listings look.

### Rate Promptly

Submit your rating soon after the trade is marked complete, while the experience is fresh. There is no expiry on rating, but timely feedback is more useful to the community.

### Be Honest, Not Harsh

The goal is accurate information for future traders, not punishment. A 3-star rating with a clear explanation is more useful than a 1-star rating without context.

### Separate Issues

If the book was great but communication was slow, say both. If communication was excellent but condition was misrepresented, say both. Nuanced comments help future traders weigh specific risks.

### Do Not Rate Before the Exchange Is Complete

Only rate after you have physically received and inspected your book. Marking a trade complete and rating before receiving the book removes your recourse if something goes wrong.

## Viewing Ratings

### On a Trader's Profile

Every user's public profile shows:

- **Average star rating** — calculated across all completed trades where they have been rated.
- **Total rating count** — the number of individual ratings received.
- **Rating history** — each rating listed chronologically, showing the star value, comment (if any), rater's name, and date.

```
┌──────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│  Jordan's Profile                            │
│                                              │
│  ★★★★☆  4.2 · 15 ratings                    │
├──────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│  Ratings                                     │
│                                              │
│  ★★★★★  Alex · 3 days ago                   │
│  Fast response and book was perfect.         │
│                                              │
│  ★★★★☆  Sam · 2 weeks ago                   │
│  Good trade. Slight delay in responding      │
│  but book was as described.                  │
│                                              │
│  ★★★★★  Morgan · 1 month ago                │
│  Smooth meetup. Would trade again.           │
└──────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

Ratings are publicly visible — no account is required to view them.

### On Your Own Profile

Visit your own profile to see ratings others have left for you. Use this to understand how other traders perceive your communication and listing accuracy, and to identify areas to improve.

## Troubleshooting

### The rating button is not showing

Rating is only available when:

1. The trade status is **completed** — confirm under **My Trades** that the trade shows the completed status badge.
2. You have not already rated this trade.

If the trade is still **accepted**, click **Mark as Complete** first — but only after the physical exchange has happened.

### "Comment is required for ratings of 3 stars or lower"

A comment is mandatory when you select 1, 2, or 3 stars. Write a brief explanation of the issue and then submit. This rule is enforced to ensure low ratings are substantiated.

### "You have already rated this trade"

Each party can submit exactly one rating per trade. If you have already rated this trade, the form will not accept a second submission. Open the trade detail to see the rating you previously submitted.

### I submitted the wrong rating

Ratings cannot be edited or deleted once submitted. If you made a genuine error (for example, clicked the wrong star count), contact BookVerse support via the Help Center to report the issue.

### The trader's average did not update immediately

The average rating updates immediately after each submission. If the number appears unchanged, refresh the profile page. If it still looks incorrect after refreshing, wait a few minutes and check again — a brief caching delay is possible.
