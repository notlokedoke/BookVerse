## Creating Your Account

Navigate to the BookVerse homepage and click **Get Started** or **Sign Up** in the navigation bar. You will be taken to the registration page.

You can register using your email address or your existing Google account.

#### Step 1 — Full Name

Enter your real name (e.g., `John Smith`). This is what other users will see when you list books or propose trades.

- Required
- Can be changed later in Profile Settings

#### Step 2 — Email

Enter a valid email address you have access to. BookVerse will send a verification link to this address.

- Required
- Must be unique — if you see "An account with this email already exists", go to [Log In](/login) instead
- Your email is not shown publicly unless you enable that in privacy settings

#### Step 3 — City / Region

Type your city or region. A dropdown of suggestions will appear as you type — select your city from the list or continue typing if it does not appear.

```
  City / Region
  ┌─────────────────────────────────┐
  │ Kath                            │
  └─────────────────────────────────┘
  ┌─────────────────────────────────┐
  │ Kathmandu, Bagmati Province     │ ← select this
  │ Kathmandu Valley                │
  └─────────────────────────────────┘
```

- Required — BookVerse uses your city to connect you with local book traders
- Minimum 2 characters
- Can be changed later in Profile Settings

#### Step 4 — Password

Create a password with at least 8 characters. Click the eye icon (👁) to show or hide what you are typing.

**Password requirements:**
- Minimum 8 characters
- At least one uppercase letter
- At least one number

#### Step 5 — Confirm Password

Re-enter the same password. The field will show an error if the two passwords do not match.

#### Step 6 — Terms Agreement

Check the box to confirm you have read and agree to the [Terms of Service](/terms) and [Privacy Policy](/privacy). Registration is not possible without this.

#### Step 7 — Submit

Click **Create Account**. The button changes to **Creating Account...** while your registration is processed.

**On success**, you will see:

```

│  ✅ Registration successful!            │
│  Please check your email to verify      │
│  your account.                          │
│                                         │
│  Redirecting to login in 4 seconds...   │
└─────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

You will be automatically redirected to the login page after 4 seconds.

### Option B: Sign Up with Google

If you prefer not to create a separate password, click **Continue with Google** on the registration page.

```
  ┌─────────────────────────────────┐
  │  G  Continue with Google        │
  └─────────────────────────────────┘
```

**What happens:**

1. You are redirected to Google's sign-in page
2. Select or sign into your Google account
3. Google redirects you back to BookVerse
4. If this is your first time, you land on the **Complete Profile** page (see [Completing Your Profile](#completing-your-profile-google-users))
5. If you have signed in with Google before, you go directly to your Dashboard

> **Note:** Google sign-in does not require email verification — your Google account is already verified.

## Verifying Your Email

> This step applies only to **email registration**. Google sign-in users skip this step.

After registering, check your inbox for an email from BookVerse with the subject line similar to:

```
Verify your BookVerse account
```

**Step 1** — Open the email and click the **Verify Email Address** button or link.

**Step 2** — You are taken to the BookVerse verification page:

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────┐
│                                     │
│        ⟳  Verifying Email          │
│                                     │
│  Please wait while we verify your   │
│  email address...                   │
│                                     │
└─────────────────────────────────────┘
```

**Step 3** — On success:

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────┐
│                                     │
│          ✅  Email Verified!        │
│                                     │
│  Your account has been verified.    │
│                                     │
│  Redirecting to login page...       │
│                                     │
└─────────────────────────────────────┘
```

You are automatically redirected to login after 3 seconds.

### Verification Link Expired?

If the link has expired or you did not receive the email:

1. On the verification error screen, click **Resend Verification Email**

   ```
   ┌─────────────────────────────────────┐
   │          ✗  Verification Failed     │
   │                                     │
   │  The link may have expired.         │
   │                                     │
   │  [ Resend Verification Email ]      │
   │  [ Back to Login ]                  │
   └─────────────────────────────────────┘
   ```

2. Enter your email address and click **Send**

3. Check your inbox for a new verification email

Alternatively, log in to your account and go to **Settings → Account**, then click **Resend Verification Email**.

Once logged in, go to **Settings** (accessible from the navigation menu or your avatar in the top right) to complete your profile.

```

├── Profile        ← name, city, bio
├── Privacy        ← visibility controls
├── Notifications  ← email preferences
└── Account        ← password, delete account
```

**Full Name** — Update your display name (2–100 characters, required).

**City / Region** — Change your location. The city selector will suggest options as you type.

**Bio** — Write a short description of your reading interests (optional, max 500 characters). This appears on your public profile and helps other traders know what genres you enjoy.

**Saving:** Click **Save Changes** or press `Cmd+S` / `Ctrl+S`. A success animation confirms the save.

**Discarding:** Click **Discard** or press `Escape` to revert unsaved changes.

### Notifications Section

Control which email notifications you receive:

| Notification | Default | Description |
|---|---|---|
| Trade Proposals | On | Email when someone proposes a trade with you |
| Messages | On | Email when you receive a new message |
| Completed Trades | On | Email when a trade is marked complete |
| Wishlist Matches | On | Email when a book on your wishlist becomes available |

Toggle each setting on or off and save.

## What's Next

After your account is set up, here is what to do first:

1. **List your first book** — Go to **My Books → Add Book** and enter the title, author, condition, and a photo of the book you want to trade
2. **Browse available books** — Go to **Browse** to see books listed by other traders in your area and globally
3. **Add books to your Wishlist** — On any book detail page, click **Add to Wishlist** so BookVerse can notify you when a match becomes available
4. **Propose a trade** — On a book detail page, click **Propose Trade** to offer one of your books in exchange

See the [Help Center](/help) for guides on each of these features.

## Troubleshooting

### "An account with this email already exists"

You already have a BookVerse account with that email. Go to the [Login page](/login) instead. If you have forgotten your password, click **Forgot Password** on the login screen.

### Verification email not received

1. Check your **Spam** or **Junk** folder
2. Confirm the email address you entered during registration is correct
3. Wait up to 5 minutes — delivery can be delayed
4. Use the **Resend Verification Email** option on the verification error page, or from **Settings → Account**

### "Please enter a valid city or region"

The city field requires at least 2 characters. If your city does not appear in the suggestions, type the full name and select the closest matching option, or type the name without selecting from the dropdown.

### Google sign-in redirects to an error page

- Make sure you are using a personal Google account (not a Google Workspace account with restricted third-party app access)
- Try clearing your browser cookies and attempting sign-in again
- If the problem persists, use the email registration option instead

### "Your session has expired. Please sign in again."

This appears on the Complete Profile page when the OAuth token in the URL has timed out. Go back to the login page and sign in with Google again — the Complete Profile step will reappear.

### Forgot your password

1. Go to the [Login page](/login)
2. Click **Forgot Password**
3. Enter your registered email address
4. Check your inbox for a password reset link
5. Click the link and set a new password
